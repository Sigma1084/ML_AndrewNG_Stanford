<blockquote>
<p><strong>Machine Learning and Data</strong><br>
“It’s not who has the best algorithm that wins. It’s who has the most data.”</p>
</blockquote>
<h3 id="note">Note</h3>
<p>When learning with large datasets (say m = 100,000,000), it’s better to experiment with a smaller dataset (say m = 1,000) first and check the model. If the model has <strong>high bias</strong>, it’s better to add new features, more training examples won’t help.</p>
